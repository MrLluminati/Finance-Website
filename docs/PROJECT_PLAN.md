# Project Plan

## 1. Working Product Definition

This project is a finance intelligence platform. It is inspired by large finance portals, but it should not begin as a direct clone of any existing website.

The first version will focus on:

- financial education;
- beginner-friendly explainers;
- clean market/news layout;
- calculators;
- curated company pages;
- watchlist and portfolio UI planning;
- legal and compliance-safe positioning.

## 2. Core Goal

Build a serious, trust-first finance platform that can serve three purposes at once:

1. learning full-stack development;
2. building a strong technology portfolio before 2028;
3. creating the foundation for a long-term finance SaaS/business.

## 3. Product Principles

### Trust First

Every claim should be traceable, explainable, and clearly separated from opinion.

### No Hype

Avoid exaggerated market language, guaranteed returns, panic headlines, or manipulative finance content.

### Education Before Recommendation

The product should teach users how to understand finance rather than telling them what to buy or sell.

### Legally Safer Growth

Do not scrape restricted financial data. Do not use real-time market data commercially without proper licensing. Do not publish regulated research or investment advice without proper compliance review.

### Build Small, Then Compound

Start with static/curated content and free tools. Add accounts, data, and SaaS features gradually.

## 4. Target Users

### Primary Initial Users

- beginner investors;
- students;
- young professionals;
- people learning personal finance;
- people who want clean explanations of markets and companies.

### Future Users

- active investors;
- long-term portfolio holders;
- analysts;
- finance bloggers;
- retail traders;
- small finance communities;
- global users seeking simplified financial intelligence.

## 7. Suggested Brand Positioning

A clean, beginner-friendly financial intelligence platform that explains markets, companies, personal finance, and financial regulation without hype.

Possible positioning statement:

> Finance intelligence made clear, credible, and beginner-friendly.

## 8. Not in Scope for Early Versions

- real-time NSE/BSE price streaming;
- paid data integrations;
- trading execution;
- broker integrations;
- buy/sell/hold recommendations;
- target prices;
- copied Moneycontrol UI/content;
- scraping exchange websites;
- complex options trading analytics.

## 9. Success Metrics

### Learning Metrics

- responsive UI;

- routing and content system working;

### Product Metrics

- repeat users;
- calculator usage;
- newsletter signups;
